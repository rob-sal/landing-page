About me
Hello! I'm Carlos Salmerón, a passionate photographer with roots in beautiful Mexico. Since I held my first camera, I knew my destiny was to capture unique moments and turn them into everlasting memories. I grew up immersed in the rich culture and vibrant life of my country, which has given me a unique perspective to capture beauty and authenticity in every image I create. Through my lens, I seek to tell stories, capture genuine emotions, and celebrate the wonderful diversity that surrounds us. I invite you to explore my world through the magic of photography. Welcome!
